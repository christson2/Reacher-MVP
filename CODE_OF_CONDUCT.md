# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment of any kind
- Sexist, racist, or otherwise discriminatory language or behavior
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Any form of unwanted contact or advances

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [email]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Consequences

Depending on the severity, consequences may include:
- A warning
- Temporary ban from participation
- Permanent removal from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, discussions, and team communications.
